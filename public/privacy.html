Nobody. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

How long we keep it

Until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or 24 months after our last contact with you, whichever comes first.

Your rights

To exercise any right, email hello@dropsynth.app — we respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This site uses no tracking cookies by default. If we add analytics or advertising pixels, this policy and a consent banner will be updated first.
